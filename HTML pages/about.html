About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is an intelligent agricultural decision support system designed to predict the most suitable crop based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions. The system uses Nitrogen, Phosphorous, Potassium, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall values to provide reliable crop recommendations.

The project applies Machine Learning techniques to analyze agricultural datasets and generate accurate predictions. These intelligent recommendations help farmers improve crop productivity, reduce resource wastage, and make better farming decisions.

The primary objective of OptiCrop is to support sustainable agriculture by assisting farmers in selecting crops that match their soil characteristics and climatic conditions, thereby increasing yield and promoting efficient resource utilization.
